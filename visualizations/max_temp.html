Latitude and Max Temperature


Cities in nothern latitudes (0 to 80) were in spring and as the latitude increases, the temperatures decreased. Cities in southern latitudes (-60 to 0) were in the early stages of autumn and so the temperatures were still warmer based on the recency of summer. If the measurments were redone at different points in the year, the plots would return different results.
