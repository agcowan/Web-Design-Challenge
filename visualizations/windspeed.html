Latitude and Windspeed


The relationship between wind speed and latitude seems vaguely parabolic, increasing speed as latitude moves away from the equator (0). That does not mean that it is strictly relational, but more of a general observation. The wind speed varies across the globe, with high winds measured in extremes and in the middle of the globe.
